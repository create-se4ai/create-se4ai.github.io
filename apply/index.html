Apply Now

To apply please send an email to apply@se4ai.org with the following:

  • The university that you prefer to attend.
  • Short statement of your interest in the program.
  • Your CV.

Admission Criteria

The program is currently open to graduate students from Concordia University, Polytechnique Montreal, Queen's University, and University of Alberta. To be admitted, prospective trainees must be Master's or PhD students in good standing under the supervision of one of the host institution's professors, or have received advice of admission to graduate studies in an upcoming academic term.

Application Period

All applications will be reviewed and assessed on a rolling basis by our internal Recruitment and Admissions committee. Successful candidates will be notified.
