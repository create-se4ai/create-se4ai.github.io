Trainee Talks - October 16th, 2024 at 10:00AM

September 21, 2022

The first CREATE SE4AI Trainee Talks of the fall 2024 semester was held on October 16. During this session, Master's candidate Lin Ling presented her research titled "Bias Unveiled: Investigating Social Bias in LLM-Generated Code". Following her presentation, Doctoral candidate Junjie Li shared his study on "An Exploratory Study on Fine-Tuning Large Language Models for Secure Code Generation". Both trainees are under the supervision of Prof. Jinqiu Yang at Concordia University.

The webinar recording is available to view on our CREATE SE4AI YouTube channel.
